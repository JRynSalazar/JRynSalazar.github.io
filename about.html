Mystical Beings of the Philippines

Legends of the Supernatural

Introduction

The Philippines has a rich array of myths and folklore, including supernatural creatures that have intrigued and frightened people for centuries. From the Aswang to the Kapre, these mythical beings are deeply embedded in Filipino tradition. This blog is designed to preserve, explore, and share these cultural gems through in-depth articles, historical insights, and regional variations.

Our Objectives

  • To educate and inform readers about mythical creatures in Philippine folklore.
  • To conserve folklores through writing and storytelling.
  • To analyze cultural implications of myths.
  • To provide a resource hub for folklore enthusiasts.

Mission

To educate and preserve the legacy of Philippine folklore by curating comprehensive, engaging, and culturally sensitive content about mythical beings and their impact on society.

Vision

To be the leading online repository and storytelling platform for Philippine supernatural folklore, inspiring generations to learn and cherish these mystical narratives.
