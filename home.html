Introduction

The Philippines has a rich array of myths and folklore, including supernatural creatures like the Aswang and Tikbalang that have captivated generations. This blog explores these creatures through cultural analysis, stories, and historical insight — ensuring they remain alive in our collective memory.

Features

Blog Articles: Explore the origins and cultural relevance of mythical creatures.
Creature Index: A categorized list with detailed descriptions and stories.
Static Content: Fixed pages like folklore intros and thematic essays.
Multimedia Content: Illustrations and videos that bring legends to life.
Comment Section (Optional): Share your insights on the legends (planned).

Objectives

This blog aims to educate, preserve, and inspire curiosity about the mythologies of the Philippines:

  • Educate readers on legendary creatures and folklore.
  • Preserve myths through written form and cultural analysis.
  • Analyze cultural impacts of folklore and mythology.
  • Build a haven for mythology enthusiasts and researchers.
