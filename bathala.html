Bathala


Overview: Bathala is the supreme god in Philippine mythology, especially in the Tagalog pantheon. Often considered the creator of the universe, Bathala is the ruler of all gods and has power over life and death. He is revered as a benevolent figure and protector of the people.

P hysical Description

  • Usually depicted as a powerful, regal figure
  • Often shown with a golden crown or holding a scepter
  • May be symbolized by the sun or other celestial objects

Abilities

  • Creator of the world and all living beings
  • Has control over life, death, and destiny
  • Can shape the heavens and the earth with his will

Weaknesses

  • Bathala is rarely seen in physical form, but his influence can be felt through natural events
  • Some stories depict Bathala's power as being limited by other gods or cosmic forces

Cultural Significance

Bathala is central to Tagalog cosmology and religious practices, representing the ultimate divine force that governs the universe. His story is used to explain natural phenomena and the origin of mankind.
